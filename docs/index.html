Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Simple tools that make your writing better in seconds.

Fix Grammar

Corrects spelling, grammar, and punctuation while keeping your voice and style intact.

Rephrase

Rewrites your text to sound more natural and fluent while preserving the meaning.

Tone Control

Switch between formal, casual, or shorter styles with a single click.

Translate

Translate between English, Arabic, French, and Spanish directly in any text field.

Always On

Lives in your menubar. Works in Slack, Gmail, WhatsApp, Notes, or any app.

Your API Key

Uses your own OpenAI key. No subscription, no data stored, fully private.
